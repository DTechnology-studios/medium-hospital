We Have Medicare Plan Options for You!

"Find the Right Medicare Plan for Your Needs and Budget." "Explore Medicare Coverage Options Tailored Just for You." "Simple, Clear Medicare Plans — Choose What’s Right for You." "Your Medicare, Your Way — Compare Plans Today." "Get the Medicare Coverage You Deserve — All in One Place."
  • Original Medicare (Parts A & B) Part A – Hospital Insurance: Covers inpatient hospital stays, skilled nursing facility care, hospice, and some home health care. Part B – Medical Insurance: Covers doctor visits, outpatient care, preventive services, and medical supplies. Who it's best for: People who want broad access to any doctor or hospital that accepts Medicare.
  • Medicare Advantage (Part C) Offered by private insurance companies approved by Medicare. Combines Parts A & B, and usually includes Part D (prescription drug coverage). Often includes extra benefits like vision, dental, and hearing. May have provider networks (like HMO or PPO plans). Who it's best for: Those looking for bundled coverage and additional benefits with potentially lower premiums.
  • Medicare Part D (Prescription Drug Plans) Standalone drug plans that work with Original Medicare. Offered by private insurers. Each plan has a formulary (list of covered drugs) and tiered pricing. Who it's best for: Anyone with Original Medicare who wants help covering prescription drug costs.
  • Medicare Supplement Insurance (Medigap) Sold by private companies to help cover "gaps" in Original Medicare (like copayments, coinsurance, and deductibles). Cannot be used with Medicare Advantage. Several plan types (Plan A, B, C, D, F, G, K, L, M, N), each with different levels of coverage. Who it's best for: People on Original Medicare who want predictable out-of-pocket costs
